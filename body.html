organic food Discription :

Organic food refers to agricultural products grown and processed without the use of synthetic chemicals such as pesticides, herbicides, and synthetic fertilizers.It follows strict
organic farming practices that prioritize environmental sustainability, animal welfare, and natural methods of pest control. Organic foods are free from genetically
modified organisms (GMOs) and artificial additives, making them a healthier choice for consumers who seek natural, wholesome, and eco-friendly food options.
